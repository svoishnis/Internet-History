    Accomplishments That Helped Make The Internet Possible

    • JCR Licklider

      • - “Father of Information Theory”
        - Invented the mathematics or theoretical groundwork for a way that a message could self-correct itself and check if the rest of the message is correct.
        - Rather than send one long message we send the in small portions.
        - This was the functional theory for packet-switching

    • Robert Elien Kham

      • - Robert Elien Kham made the first hardware in 1965 – 1969 and sent the first packet using these self-correcting messages.
        - ARPANET was a direct result of this technology that focused solving two of the main issues
        - With circuit switching, if the wire was cut then there is no way to send the message over to confirm there was no loss of information or tampering.
        - The desire to send data rather than just a voice.
        - By 1972, the ARPANET composed of 37 companies including universities, research collaborators, and government labs.

    • Bill Gates

      • - Co-founder and CEO of Microsoft
        - Bill Gates was able to commission an operating system for IBM from an electronics company and made it work on their computers
        - Created the MSN – the Microsoft security network thought the internet was like a toll road
        - Feared the growth of Netscape to change the foundation of the Internet where the browsers could hold the power

    • Al Gore

      • - Senator and sponsor of the bill that made the ARPANET public
        - The Internet became available in August 6th, 1991
